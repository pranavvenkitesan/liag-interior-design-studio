How we work

Five phases, one throughline.

Phase 01

We begin with the site and the brief — studying orientation, climate, code, and budget. Before a single line is drawn, we know what the building must do and why.

Phase 02

Form takes shape. Massing, volume, and relationship to site are tested through models and drawings until the core idea is undeniable and the plan resolves.

Phase 03

We coordinate structure, systems, and detail with engineers and consultants — producing the complete, code-compliant set required to secure permits.

Phase 04

We stay on site through construction administration — reviewing submittals, resolving conditions, and protecting the design intent from drawing to build.

Phase 05

Handover. The building is commissioned, documented, and delivered — and we return after a season to see how it lives, and learn for the next one.
