What we do

A studio for the whole space.

01

Full interior design

Concept, space planning, materials, lighting, and furnishing — managed end to end so you live in the finished room, not the process.

02

Renovation & remodel

Reworking what you have — walls, flow, and finishes — to make a tired space feel considered, generous, and new again.

03

Styling & furnishing

The final layer — furniture, textiles, art, and objects sourced and placed so the room reads as one quiet, complete thought.

04

Design consultation

A focused session for a single room or decision — colour, layout, or a second opinion — when you want direction, not a full project.

How we work

From first conversation to final styling.

A clear, four-step process so you always know where your project stands.

Estimate your project
  1. 01

    Discovery

    We talk through how you live, what you love, and what the space needs to do. We listen more than we pitch.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Mood, layout, and material direction — presented as a single, coherent vision you can see and feel before we build.

  3. 03

    Design & detail

    Drawings, specifications, and sourcing. Every finish, fixture, and dimension resolved before a single wall moves.

  4. 04

    Delivery & styling

    We manage the build, install the furnishings, and style the final layer — then hand you the keys to a finished room.

They understood the way we wanted to live before we could put it into words — and then built exactly that.

Ayesha & Bilal Rahman The Gulberg Residence
