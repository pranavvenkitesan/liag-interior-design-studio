How we work

Everything a space needs, from one studio.

We design buildings and the interiors inside them — and everything between. Take the whole service, or just the part you need.

01

Architecture

The building itself — how it sits on its site, holds the light, and stands up. We take a project from first concept through to a permitted, buildable set of drawings.

  • Concept & massing
  • Site & feasibility
  • Structural coordination
  • Facade & envelope
  • Permits & approvals
  • Construction drawings
02

Interiors

The life inside the walls — planned against the real architecture, not a generic shell. Every layout, material, and light decision is made with the building it lives in.

  • Space planning
  • Materials & finishes
  • Lighting design
  • Joinery & built-ins
  • Furniture selection
  • Procurement
03

Styling & furnishing

The final layer that makes a space feel finished and lived-in — sourced and placed by the same team that drew the walls, so nothing reads as an afterthought.

  • Art & objects
  • Textiles & rugs
  • Plants & greenery
  • Final dressing
  • Photography styling
  • Handover
04

Consultation

Not every project needs the full studio. Bring us in for early feasibility, a design review, or a second opinion before you commit serious budget to a direction.

  • Feasibility studies
  • Design advisory
  • Second opinions
  • Concept sketches
  • Budget guidance
  • Contractor review
The method

How a project moves.

01

Brief & discovery

We start with how you'll live or work in the space — budget, site, and the feeling you're after.

02

Concept

Sketches and massing that test the big idea before any detail is committed.

03

Design development

Architecture and interiors resolved together — drawings, materials, and light as one set.

04

Documentation

A complete, coordinated drawing set ready for permits and construction.

05

Build & oversight

We stay on through construction so the build serves the design, not the other way round.

06

Styling & handover

The final layer installed, the space dressed, and the keys handed over.

Good to know

Common questions.

Fees depend on scope, scale, and finish level. Most projects are a percentage of construction cost or a fixed fee agreed after the brief. Use the quick estimate for an indicative range, then we confirm in writing.
On full-service projects we stay on through construction to oversee that the build matches the design. We can also hand over a complete drawing set for your own contractor.
